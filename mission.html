Mission Statement

KRIPA Mission Statement

KRIPA is a forum to facilitate and enable physicians to excel in patient care, teaching and research and to pursue their aspirations in professional and community affairs.



Motto:

Unity of Purpose

Commitment to Excellence

Compassion towards Fellow-beings



Vision:

To promote a professional solidarity in the pursuit of excellence in patient care, teaching, research and community service.



Values:

Pursuit of excellence and a great commitment to an ethical conduct.

Respect for Human Dignity.

Commitment to community service.



Goals:

Provide the best patient care with compassion as a service to the community.

Maintain the highest standards of ethics and professionalism.

Help patients become fully informed and empowered partners.

Remain current and knowledgable in contemporary and anticipated legislative and regulatory changes impacting the practice of medicine.

Assert equal protection in all their professional pursuits through collective advocacy.
